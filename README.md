**squ**are sp**iral**

```
Welcome to Squiral!
Here is an example:
21 22 23 24 25
20  7  8  9 10
19  6  1  2 11
18  5  4  3 12
17 16 15 14 13
```

The basic idea behind printing this matrix is<br/>
to start from the middle of the matrix and then moving:<br/>
`right` >> `down` >> `left` >> `up`<br/>
and not returning to the same row again.

### Install

`pip install squiral`

#### Usage

```python
>>> import squiral as sq
>>> sq.printout(sq.produce(5))
21 22 23 24 25
20  7  8  9 10
19  6  1  2 11
18  5  4  3 12
17 16 15 14 13
>>>
```
